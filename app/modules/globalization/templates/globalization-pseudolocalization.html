Pseudo localization

When you write your first piece of UI or help, have the Globalization team run pseudo-localization. We can tell you right away what works and what does not.

Pseudo-localization consists in creating fake translations, generating target localized files and importing them into the build. Using language-specific characters (é, õ, ù, å, ß, 送, س,…) and expanding string lengths, we can assess in if all strings have been externalized and if your target languages display correctly. Pseudo-localization is a very important step that is carried out as part of the internationalization phase. Please ensure you add it as a task to your backlog, and assign it to the Globalization team early in the development phase.
