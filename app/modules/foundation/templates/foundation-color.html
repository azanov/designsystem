Color Palettes

Our primary color palette is designed to display well, with optimum contrast, on the web and on mobile devices. Our foundational brand colors — cyan and magenta — don’t read well in small type sizes on digital screens. So they’ve been limited to use as accent colors and large type sizes only. Our auxiliary color palette contains colors that should be used sparingly due to their potential to clash with our other two palettes.

Gradients

Our gradients are based on our primary and accent palettes and can be used to add visual interest to your product’s navigation. A gradient always consists of a combination of a primary color on the left and an accent color on the right. While this system allows for a range of options we recommend using the first gradient shown (PB Blue transitioning to PB Magenta), to keep our products looking consistent. Whichever gradient you use, make sure that its color aligns with the colors you’ve chosen for the rest of your product.
