    Typography

    Thoughtful use of typography is an anchor to our system so it never feels cluttered or jumbled. These fonts chosen have been selected for both their connection to the Pitney Bowes brand typography as well as their ability to perform well on the web. NOTE: Always use even number fonts, and never use a type size below 12 points.
