Sample Email Template

A generic template used for transactional emails within products.

The scenarios outlined below can be used as a guidline for copy within the email template. Be sure to make the following changes within the copy blocks, here are some tips:

  • Replace all occurances of bracketed content, such as [[PRODUCT NAME]] with the appropriate copy and links
  • Pay attention to indications of register and trade marks
  • Refer to the Global support copy options at the end of this document, replace all occurances of [[GLOBAL SUPPORT COPY OPTION]] with your selected option
