Form Fields

The form field is our most often used input, and thus has a range of states and uses that inform our users. It is our rule that the content within a field is the primary design focus, while the label is secondary.

Basic Form Fields

We have three sizes of form fields: small, default and large. The default size should be used for fields in all instances except:

  • With 20+ fields; use only small fields (Example: full page of entry fields)
  • With nine or less fields; use large fields (Example: sign in screens)
