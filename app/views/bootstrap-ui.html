Bootstrap UI More Info

The angular-ui bootstrap components require the design_system_bootstrap_ui.css stylesheet to be included in your project. Due to limitations in the bootstrap-ui directives and templates, additional classes may be required on components for proper styling using ng-class.

Some variations of components may not be available and are noted in the examples below.
